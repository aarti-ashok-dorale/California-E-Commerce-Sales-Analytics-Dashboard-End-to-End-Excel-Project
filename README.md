# California-E-Commerce-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-End-to-End-Excel-Project(VIVA CALIF)
End-to-End Excel Business Intelligence Project

# Project Overview

This project demonstrates the complete data analysis lifecycle using Microsoft Excel, from raw data preparation to interactive dashboard creation.

The objective of this project is to analyze e-commerce transactions across California and provide business insights through data visualization and KPI reporting.

# The entire dashboard is built using:

Slicers
KPI Cards
Conditional Formatting
Geographic Visualization

# Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large amounts of transactional data every day. However, raw data alone does not provide actionable insights.

This project converts raw transaction data into an interactive dashboard that answers key business questions:

How are sales performing over time?
Which products are most popular?
How many items do customers typically purchase?
Which channels do customers prefer for purchasing?
Where are customers located?
How quickly are products delivered?
How satisfied are customers?

# Key Business Insights
📌 Sales Performance
Sales remained relatively stable across the 13-week period with a few noticeable peaks.
Revenue and quantity trends move closely together, indicating consistent average order values.

📌 Customer Purchase Behavior
Most customers purchase between 1 and 3 items per order.
Orders containing more than 10 items are relatively rare.

📌 Purchase Channel Preferences
Mobile applications and websites contribute significantly to customer purchases.
Social channels such as Instagram contribute a smaller share of total purchases.

📌 Product Performance
T-Shirts, Jeans, and Sneakers are among the top-selling products.
Jewelry and niche products contribute the least to total sales.

📌 Geographic Distribution
Customer concentration is highest in major California cities, especially Southern California regions.

📌 Delivery Performance
Most orders are delivered within 1 to 3 days, indicating efficient logistics operations.

📌 Customer Satisfaction
The average customer rating is 4.0/5, suggesting generally positive customer experiences.
